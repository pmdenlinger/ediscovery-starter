### Project manager and legal team should inquire client about each of the following sources:

- [ ] Computer workstation or laptop hard drives (Windows, Mac, Linux)

- [ ] Internal and external email systems (Outlook, Gmail, Yahoo, iCloud, etc.)

- [ ] Personal or departmental directories or file shares on company servers (for each custodian and business unit)

- [ ] Database servers or proprietary systems and applications

- [ ] Peripheral devices and network attached devices

- [ ] Business and personal smartphones and mobile devices

- [ ] Home computers and laptops if used for business purposes

- [ ] Web servers, corporate webpages, and/or social media sites

- [ ] Collaboration platforms (Zoom, Slack, Microsoft Teams, etc.)

- [ ] ESI stored in any cloud-based applications

- [ ] Web-based news feeds and Bloomberg messages

- [ ] External storage devices (CD/DVDs, flash drives, external drives)

- [ ] Voicemail systems (if retained)

- [ ] Archive, backup, compliance retention, and disaster recovery systems

- [ ] Retired legacy systems
