### Contents checklist
*[ ] Brief summary of nature and circumstances of dispute
*[ ] Statement of legal obligation to preserve relevant information
*[ ] Directive not to delete, alter or destry any potentially relevant documents and ESI
*[ ] Sources and types of documents to be preserved, how to properly handle them, and consequences for failing to do so
*[ ] Request that each recipient of the litigation hold acknowledgement receipt of the notice
*[ ] A person to contact if there are any questions

### Notification and confirmation
*[ ] Obtain confirmation from each custodian that he/she has read and understood the legal hold notice
*[ ] Coordinate with client's legal representatives and/or IT personnel to monitor the hold, circulate periodic reminders.
*[ ] Revise the scope or sources of potentially relevant ESI to be preserved when additional information is made available

### When litigation has ended
*[ ] Release the litigation hold so that the organization may resume its standard retention practices
